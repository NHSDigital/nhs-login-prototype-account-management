Subj: Information about your NHS login

Hello

You have not logged in to your NHS login in the last 12 months.

If you do not log in within the next 12 months, your account will be closed.

You can use NHS login to access a range of health and care websites and apps wherever you see the NHS login button.
To view all services using NHS login visit: https://www.nhs.uk/nhs-services/online-services/nhs-login/websites-and-apps-you-can-access-with-nhs-login/

By logging in to the NHS App or one of these services in the next month, you will keep your account open.

This means you can view your health records, connect with your GP, book appointments and order repeat prescriptions amongst other things.
