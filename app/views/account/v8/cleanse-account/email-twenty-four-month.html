We are closing your NHS login

Dear Jonathan Kingston

You have not logged into an online health service using your NHS login in the last 2 years.

To keep your login details, you will need to access a service that uses NHS login. You need to do this within the next 30 days.

You can use your NHS login to:

  • view your health records
  • connect with your GP
  • book appointments
  • order repeat prescriptions
  • access a range of health and care websites, apps and services with one set of login details

To find out more about the services you can access with NHS login, visit: https://www.nhs.uk/nhs-services/online-services/nhs-login/websites-and-apps-you-can-access-with-nhs-login/

Help with NHS login

  • If you need help registering or you are having technical issues visit the NHS login help centre: https://help.login.nhs.uk/
  • Change your NHS login details and password or delete your NHS login if you no longer want to use it: https://settings.login.nhs.uk/
